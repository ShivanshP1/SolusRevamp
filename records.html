Tax Forms

Tax forms for the current and previous years are available for download below. These forms include:

T2202 - Tuition and Enrolment Certificate
Official tax receipt for tuition paid and months enrolled
T4A - Scholarship Income
Report of scholarship/bursary income received
RL-8 (Québec Residents)
Tuition and education tax credit for Québec

Important Tax Information

  • T2202 forms are typically available by the end of February each year
  • You can claim tuition fees paid for the current year and any unused amounts carried forward from previous years
  • Keep all tax documents for at least 6 years
  • For questions about tax forms, contact Student Accounts
